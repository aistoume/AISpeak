The prelearned words are stored in WordList.txt, by learn order.
The frequency characteristic is stored in Characteristic.txt. Each row corresponds to the word in the same row in WordList. The first 10 number are the normalized amplitude of 20,50,100,200,500,1000,2000,5000,10000,20000. The following 20 number are the pair of peak frequency and normalized amplitude at that frequency, sorted according to the amplitude.
